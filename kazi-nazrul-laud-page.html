Kazi Nazrul Islam

Poet, Musician, Revolutionary

About Kazi Nazrul Islam

Kazi Nazrul Islam (1899-1976) was a Bengali poet, musician, and revolutionary who is considered one of the greatest literary figures in Bengali literature. He was also a political activist and played an important role in the Indian independence movement.

Works

  • Rebel (Bidrohi)
  • The Agony of the Ganges (Padma Nadir Majhi)
  • The Call of the Marching Bell (Bisher Bashi)

Legacy

Kazi Nazrul Islam's poetry and music continue to inspire people around the world. He is often referred to as the "Rebel Poet" and his works reflect his revolutionary spirit and commitment to social justice.
